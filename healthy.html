Key Points for a Healthy Lifestyle

1. Balanced Diet

A balanced diet provides essential nutrients to the body. Eating healthy food improves immunity and energy levels.

  • Eat fruits and vegetables daily
  • Avoid junk and oily food
  • Drink plenty of water

2. Regular Physical Activity

Exercise keeps the body active and reduces the risk of diseases. Even simple activities help maintain fitness.

  • Walk or jog daily
  • Play outdoor games
  • Practice yoga

3. Mental Well-being

Mental health affects how we think and behave. A calm mind helps in better decision-making and happiness.

  • Practice meditation
  • Talk to family and friends
  • Avoid stress and negativity

4. Proper Sleep
